What is GDP?

Gross Domestic Product (GDP) is the total monetary value of all goods and services produced within a country's borders in a specific time period. It serves as a comprehensive measure of a nation's overall economic activity. America’s GDP has changed a lot over time, and America has become one of the world’s strongest powers.
